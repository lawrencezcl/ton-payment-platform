TON Foundation x Ignyte Hackathon: Chain the Future ChallengeTONTokenizationWeb3CryptoPaymentsThe TON x Ignyte Global Challenge is designed to accelerate the adoption of Web3 payments as a natural part of everyday life inside Telegram — the world’s most dynamic social platform. This global program invites innovators, startups, and scale-ups to build Web3 products for Business, Payments, and Tokenization.From splitting bills with friends, to building plug-and-play tools for merchants to reimagine ownership and value by tokenizing assets, the challenge seeks to reimagine how TON can unlock the next wave of mainstream crypto adoption. Winning teams will gain access to TON’s ecosystem, mentorship from industry leaders, and opportunities to pilot their solutions with global communities of users, creators, and enterprises.Problem StatementThemeProblem StatementFocus AreasCrypto infrastructure for businessEmpower businesses to go fully on-chain.
